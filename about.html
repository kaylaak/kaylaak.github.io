About Me

Hey There!

I'm Kayla Kraft, a recent graduate from Lehigh University with a degree in Computer Science and Business. During my time at Lehigh, I discovered I have a passion for working for clients and using different techonology to help create solutions for them.

For the past two summers, I've had the opportunity to intern as a Software Engineer at Microsoft in Redmond, WA. During Summer 2022, I was placed on the Web XT Edge Performance. This is where I was able to work in C/C++ to improve the user's experience on the Edge browser. During Summer of 2023, I was on the Automanage Machine Configuration team, where I created an Extension Repair Tool in C++ and Go to enhance the Azure Arc user experience.

As the head Teaching Assistant for Introduction to Programming over three semesters, I discovered my passion for helping others. After graduation, I launched my own tutoring services, offering personalized 1:1 and group lessons. Through this, I've helped students to grasp programming concepts and create a deeper understanding of the subject.

When I'm not coding, you'll often find me hanging out with my dog, Kookie, or going for long walks to get my daily steps in. I'm also a huge foodie! I love exploring new restaurants and cuisines, and I LOVE sharing my culinary adventures on Yelp.

Looking ahead, I'm excited about the possibilities in the tech industry. Currently, I am interested in becoming a Product Manager or a Tech Consultant, where I can continue to learn, grow, and work with different clients of all backgrounds.

Let's connect and create something amazing together!
