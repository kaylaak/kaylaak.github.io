About Me!

  Let me introduce myself to you. I am a 19 year old student who grew up in a small town in Long Island for my entire life. I have a dog, named Kookie, and she is the star of my world. Other then spending time with my dog, I plan on getting a B.S. in Computer Science and Business at Lehigh University. To be honest, I have tried to change my major multiple times. Every time I signed the major declaration form, I can never sign my name on the line. How I got into coding is another story.
I received my first computer in kindergarten, and ever since then a computer has always been by my side. In 11th grade, I accidentally signed up for AP Computer Science Principles, and surprisingly didn't drop out on the first day. I didn't drop out at all, even when I was failing every test. My CS teacher told me if I wanted him to write my recommendation letter, I would need to sign up for the next CS class. Fast forward a couple months after senior year, on all of my applications, I clicked "Computer Science" as my intended major. And now here I am!
